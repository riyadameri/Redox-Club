About Redox Club

Our Team at Tech Conference 2023

Empowering Students Through Technology

Redox Club is the premier student organization at Kasdi Merbah University, proudly supported by Redox Institution, dedicated to artificial intelligence, technology, media, and innovation.

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to develop technical skills, collaborate on projects, and prepare for future careers in tech-related fields with the backing of Redox Institution's resources and expertise.

Founded in 2020, we have grown to become one of the most active student clubs at KMU Ouargla, with over 55 members across multiple departments and teams, all benefiting from Redox Institution's support. Under the leadership of Chaima Gougi
